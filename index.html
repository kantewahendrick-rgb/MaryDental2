The People Behind Your Smile

Meet Our Team

Experienced, caring professionals dedicated to your comfort and confidence at every visit.

Dr Mary
B.Ch.D · Principal Dentist & Founder

The founder of Mary Dental Practice, Dr Mary built the practice on one simple belief: dental care should be comfortable, convenient and personal. With a Bachelor of Dental Surgery and a deep passion for her community, she established the Wanaheda practice to bring expert, compassionate dentistry to Windhoek families. Known for her gentle chair-side manner and meticulous clinical technique, Dr Mary has built a loyal following of patients who trust her completely. She is committed to making quality dental care accessible and comfortable for every Namibian.

🦷 Principal Dentist & Founder
Dr Hendrick
B.Ch.D · Associate Dentist

Dr Hendrick joined Mary Dental Practice bringing fresh expertise and a specialisation in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. With post-graduate training in aesthetic dentistry and digital smile design, he excels in veneers, composite bonding and orthodontic treatment. Known for his natural rapport with patients and families, Dr Hendrick combines cutting-edge clinical technique with the warm, unhurried approach the practice is loved for. Patients consistently praise his thoroughness and ability to explain procedures in plain, reassuring language.

✨ Cosmetic & Restorative
🤝
Support Team
Nurses · Assistants · Reception

Our clinical and administrative team is the true heart of the practice. From the moment you call to book to the moment you leave, our dental nurses, chair-side assistants and front-desk staff ensure your visit is smooth, caring and professional. They handle appointments, medical aid queries and emergency scheduling with warmth and efficiency every single day, ensuring every patient feels genuinely welcomed at Mary Dental Practice.
